Arizona, USA

Arizona, in the southwestern U.S., is famous for its desert landscapes, including the Grand Canyon. It became the 48th state in 1912 and is home to Native American tribes like the Navajo. Phoenix, the capital, has some of the hottest U.S. temperatures, while its mountains offer cooler climates and skiing. Arizona is also a leading copper producer.
